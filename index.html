Better Business English Seminar
商務英語進修班

SEMINAR

近來，不少僱主投訴港人英語水平每下愈況，辭不達意之餘，基本文法亦錯漏百出。想要在職場甘更進一步，良好的英語能力可謂不可或缺。有見及此，我們推出了這個「速效英語工作坊」，教導不同學院的參加者，掌握正常的商務英語。

DETAIL

1. Review basic English sentence structure from the most simple to the more complex ones,

2. Use the limitations inherent in the sentence structure and English semantics (diction) to illustrate the differences in the thinking process and thereby thought-pattern between native and non-native English speakers,

3. Train the participants to learn to think like a native if they were to avoid writing Chinese English, not to mention having a good command of English,

4. Cite examples of Chinese English vs. proper English usage in an effort to avoid writing Chinese or improper English,

5. Discuss major common pitfalls committed by non-native speakers and the recommended remedies,

6. Discuss major elements of good business English.

7. Review a past/current English paper of ≤950 words (e.g. a speech, statement of purpose, job application letter, or others) written by each participant on a topic of his/her choice.
